Pamphlet design

A series of briefing notes created for South 32 called “MAATLAFATSO”

The first series of pamphlets and the digital screens that accompany them

This series of pamphlets were designed as part of a housing scheme initiative for the mining industry. The brief asked me to create an entire look and feel for the series which would be at once striking and easy to read and understand. The statistics were integrated into the design of the pamphlets in such a way as to be bold and readable. Alongside each series of printed material was a deck of screens for digital and social media purposes, which broke up the information into smaller parts while maintaining the same look and feel.
