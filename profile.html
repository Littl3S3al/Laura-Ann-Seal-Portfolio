ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Laura and I like to think of myself as a self-taught do it all woman. A digital maker, artist, coder and thinker; I am constantly looking for exciting new ways to learn, show my creativity and share my passion. I work as a freelance web designer, developer and graphic designer while I complete my Masters in Game Design part time.

AWARDS & ACHIEVMENTS

Summa Cum Laude | Top Student in Digital Arts 2018 For the completion of my Honours in Interactive Digital Media.
1st Prize [Diversity & Narrative Categories] SA Game Jam 2018 My team of 3 designed and built a game called “Sole Food” for the competition which was awarded 2 winning spots.
Fakugesi “Digital Spirit of Jozi” finalist My augmented reality mobile application was selected to represent Fakugesi at the Digital Spirit Exhibition in 2018 – ZKN Museum, Germany.
