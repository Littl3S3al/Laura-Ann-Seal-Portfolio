Experimental Website

Net Neutrality: What does the advent of the internet mean for digital social culture

This website is an experimental one, in terms of user experience. It creates a three-dimensional world in which the user is invited to play and experiment within the site. The goal is to gain some understanding of what major websites are at play in the digital realm and how they rank and influence the age of the internet.

A 3D sphere is built using three,js coding library wherein websites are dynamically ordered according to their global user reach. By clicking on each site the user is able to read information about the website and gauge the statistics of its user base. There are a number of starry outliers which can be interacted with within the space, which provide the user with information regarding net neutrality and how the dominance of certain websites affects our internet culture at large.
