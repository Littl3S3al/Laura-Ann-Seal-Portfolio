Day Spa Website

A website for the Urban Health Concepts day spa

The brief here was to create a website using the existing corporate identity of the UHC day spa (their green colour and their logo) whilst incorporating something bold, luxurious and exciting. I used big hero images to highlight the experiential aspect of visiting the day spa and brought in an interesting pop of pink. This website serves as a great platform to show off the numerous services and products on offer at Urban Health Concepts.
