media pitch

I worked with Meropa Communications to make their pitch to Transnet come alive with graphics, drawings and media content.

Brief: MEROPA ADVERTISING intends to roll out an 18-month campaign that changes the way South Africa and the world look at Transnet. The first phase of the campaign was a roadshow for internal communications. For this, I created renders and 3D mock-ups of what this roadshow would look like.

UNDERNEATH: The roadshow is built up of shipping containers painted in the Transnet colours, with each container housing a different set of information about Transnet: the past, the present and the future of the company.

The second phase of the campaign was to have a work force representing Transnet called “The Green Ants” whose task it was to complete acts of service for the country.

The uniform design for the green ants.

The billboard advertising the green ants

Part of the campaign were a series of video adverts which were designed to publicise the good work being done by Transnet.

BELOW: The first Transnet TVC will introduce Transnet to South Africa as a business wanting to make change. This commercial will act as an introduction to the public of Transnets plan to turn their reputation around by encouraging the public to have their say. Transnet will make a commitment to start listening.

BELOW: The objective for the second Transnet TVC is to introduce The Green Ants to South Africa as the ‘every person’. The Green Ants are Transnet’s employees from intern to CEO. Below is a short summary of the creative direction for “The Green Ants are All Of Us” above the line advertisement.

BELOW: The objective for “The Transnet Journey” TVC’s is to depict a unique journey in each advert of a raw material, transported by Transnet via Freight, to the material’s inception into everyday life.
