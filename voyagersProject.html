Voyagers Travel Company

Pitch for re-branding the corporate identity of the company

PHOTOGRAPHY | COLOUR | DESIGN:

This was the proposed brand initiative. It was my plan to take the Voyagers brand right through the process of developing a new brand identity and marketing strategy which remains true to the core values of the company. This included the marketing and messaging; the logo re-design; the photography and colour scheme; the corporate identity and website look and feel.

BRANDING THOUGHTS:

  • Voyager is an African company; we are targeting African clients and businesses.
  • We should act to make our messaging unique to this selling point.

REPRESENTATION OF AFRICA:

  • Avoid the same old – and here’s why: everyone already knows it; it doesn’t need reinforcement. Layer exciting new stories over existing brand memory.
  • Empowering Africa as vibrant and exciting.

WHAT TO EXPECT FROM THIS PITCH?

  • Empowering marketing strategy which is exciting and enticing.
  • A unique edge over competitors.
  • A focus on appealing to the identities and values of our target market.
  • A brand which stays true to the values of the Voyager company.
  • A powerful and homogenous brand identity which can incorporate all contributing brands.
  • A branding strategy which can grow with the company; is easily able to expand whilst remaining true to the overall identity of Voyagers.
